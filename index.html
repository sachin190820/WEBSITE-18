Maciej

pronounced 'much-yay'

I’m a design generalist and my work spans across multiple disciplines from product design to branding, engineering and art. I went down the design rabbit hole doing things on the internet like flash websites and Mac themes and after graduating in graphic design in 2010 I started my own studio.

Since then I’ve been lucky to work with brands big and small, teams of two and companies of thousands, on projects that spanned the many disciplines of contemporary design.

In my design work I pursue the visceral connections we form with digital products. I create opinionated, fun interfaces that invite people to manipulate and play and brands that embrace their communities. My formal education in music made me appreciate the rhythm and structure behind design and became a north star in my process.

I live and work from my home studio in Wrocław, Poland. I enthuse over all things design but also compose electronic music, skateboard, enjoy and make art, obsess over fashion and explore emerging technologies.

I consider myself incredibly lucky to have worked with some amazing people, who enabled and helped me do my best work. I’d like to thank y’all, you know who you are.
